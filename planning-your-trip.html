TRANSPORTATION

Guests who arrive at the Atlanta airport have several options for ground transportation.  Rental cars and taxi cabs are readily available at the airport, and rental cars are also available at the Emory Conference Center Hotel’s on-site Enterprise facility.  Atlanta’s public transportation system MARTA offers bus and rail service, but planning to rely solely on public transportation and/or taxi cabs is not recommended.  Guests who are unable to drive are asked to please contact the bride personally so that a close friend or family member can be arranged to provide ground transportation to wedding events.

DIRECTIONS

From the Atlanta airport, take I-85 North to Exit 248C, Freedom Parkway.  Follow the signs to The Carter Center and bear right onto 42 East until it dead ends.  Turn left onto Moreland Avenue which becomes Briarcliff Road.  Stay on Briarcliff Road for approximately three miles.  Turn right onto Clifton Road.  At the fifth traffic light, turn left into the Emory Conference Center Hotel.  Click the button below for a map of directions from the Atlanta airport to the Emory Conference Center Hotel.
